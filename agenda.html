Innovator's
Arena

The Ultimate Idea Pitching Event, Where innovation meets opportunity, and ideas turn into reality.

Registration and Welcome
9:00 AM - 9:30 AM

Participants will check in, receive their welcome kits, and settle into the venue. This will be followed by an introductory session where they will be briefed about the event flow and general guidelines.

Inauguration & Opening Speech
9:30 AM - 10:00 AM

The event will officially kick off with an inauguration ceremony, including a welcome address by the organizing committee and a speech from a special guest or senior faculty.

Idea Pitching (Domain 1: Artificial Intelligence)
10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Teams in the Artificial Intelligence domain will present their ideas. Each team will have 5-7 minutes to present their idea, followed by a 3-minute Q&A session with the jury.

Lunch Break
1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Lunch will be provided to all participants, jury members, and event organizers. Itâ€™s a great time for networking and discussing ideas informally.

Idea Pitching (Domain 2: Machine Learning)
2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Teams in the Machine Learning domain will showcase their innovative ideas. The same pitch format will be followed: 5-7 minutes for the presentation, and 3 minutes for Q&A.

Tea Break
5:00 PM - 5:15 PM

A short refreshment break to recharge before the final session of the day.

Idea Pitching (Domain 3: Automation)
5:15 PM - 7:15 PM

Participants in the Automation domain will pitch their ideas to the jury panel, with time allocated for questions and feedback.

Day 1 Wrap-Up and Announcements
7:15 PM - 7:30 PM

The day will conclude with a brief wrap-up, including reminders and announcements for Day 2, as well as updates on the finalists selected for the final round.

Registration for Day 2
9:00 AM - 9:30 AM

Participants and attendees for Day 2 will register and get prepared for another exciting day of idea presentations.

Idea Pitching (Domain 4: Business Model & Entrepreneurship)
9:30 AM - 12:30 PM

The focus shifts to business models and entrepreneurial ideas. Teams will pitch their solutions with the usual format: 5-7 minutes for presentations, followed by a 3-minute Q&A with the jury.

Lunch Break
12:30 PM - 1:30 PM

A break for lunch where participants, jury members, and volunteers can relax and network.

Idea Pitching (Domain 5: Software & Technology Innovation)
1:30 PM - 4:00 PM

The last domain of the event will showcase ideas related to software and technology innovation. Teams will present their solutions, following the same pitch and Q&A format.

Tea Break
4:00 PM - 4:15 PM

A short break before the final session of the event.

Announcement of Finalists
4:15 PM - 4:30 PM

One finalist will be selected from each domain (AI, Machine Learning, Automation, Business Model & Entrepreneurship, Software & Technology Innovation), and they will proceed to the final round.

Final Round (On-the-Spot Problem Statement)
4:30 PM - 6:30 PM

The 5 finalists will be given a surprise, on-the-spot problem statement. They will have 2 hours to brainstorm and propose an innovative solution. Creativity, technical expertise, and teamwork will be critical for this challenge.

Jury Deliberation
6:30 PM - 7:00 PM

The jury will review and discuss the final round presentations to determine the top three winners.

Closing Ceremony & Prize Distribution
7:00 PM - 7:30 PM

The event will conclude with the announcement of the winners. The 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place teams will receive their prizes, followed by a short closing speech by the event organizers.

KreatiCon

2 Days Ideathon!

Put on your thinking caps as you get ready for a creativity problem-solving workout. This is a two-day event where people with bright ideas will come and present their ideas in five technology segments such as AI, ML, Automation, and many others. Teams must come up with their ideas with each domain having one finalist to compete in the last round. In the last round of the competition, 5 teams will be shortlisted on the spot, and they will have only one hour to come up with a different solution. Don’t forget the attraction of attractive awards for the 1st, 2nd, and 3rd places! Do not let a day go by more so a week, a month, and a year whereby you are not translating your ideas into implementation.
